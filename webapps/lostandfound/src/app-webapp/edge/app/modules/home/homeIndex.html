Have you Secured your belongings?


        Never lose anything hence on! Secure all your belongings with us! We assure you that in case you lose something, we would try our best to connect you to the one who finds your missing item. All you need to do is to create your own 'Lost and Found Id' and add a visible note to all of your belongings which would say:

If you find me, please report it on "http://lostandfound.report/found=YOUR_ID"

This is a free service. Click here to create your 'Lost and Found Id'.
